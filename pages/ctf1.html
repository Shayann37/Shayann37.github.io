CybersecurityChallenge

The CyberSecurityChallenge Belgium is an annual competition designed to identify and develop cybersecurity talent in Belgium. Participants, including students, professionals, and enthusiasts, compete in online qualifiers and a final round to showcase their skills in various cybersecurity domains. The challenge serves as a platform for networking with industry professionals and raising awareness about cybersecurity.
I took part of the 2022 only qualifiers and got the 157th place with my team "Katre"
