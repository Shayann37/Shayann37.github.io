Hackaton 2022

In 2022 I took part at my first EPHEC's Hackaton

A hackathon is a weekend event where teams of 3 to 5 people have to develop a project during the weekend. It was the first time I participated in a hackathon. It was a very enriching experience. I was able to learn how to work with a team and manage our time.

The theme was about sustainable development.

We developed an application aimed at facilitating the buying and selling of products using geolocation services. With our platform, users can easily browse, buy, and sell a variety of items within their local area. I'm proud to share that our application garnered significant recognition, earning us the first place in the competition!
