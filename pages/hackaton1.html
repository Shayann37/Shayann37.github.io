Hackaton 2022

In 2022 I took part at my first EPHEC's Hackaton
A hackathon is a weekend event where teams of 3 to 5 people have to develop a project during the weekend.
It was the first time I participated in a hackathon. It was a very enriching experience. I was able to learn how to work with a team and manage our time.
The theme was about sustainable development. We developed a platform to exchange re-usable things such as hardware/books/etc between EPHEC students, and we won the third place!
