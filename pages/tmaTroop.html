Scout Web Page

During my years as a scout leader at TMA Troop, I took on the responsibility of maintaining the troop's website.

This role involved various tasks such as redesigning the site, adding new pages, and regularly updating information. Working with Wix, an online hosting platform, provided me with my first experience in website creation. It was an opportunity for me to delve into the world of online presence management, learning the ins and outs of maintaining a digital platform accessible to our troop members and the broader community.

This experience not only honed my technical skills but also taught me the importance of effective communication and organization in managing an online platform.
